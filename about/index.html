About Wellsplit

WellSplit is a free website of calculators and decision tools built to help people make fair shared-money decisions with more clarity and less conflict. Whether you're splitting rent with roommates, dividing bills with a partner, settling trip expenses with friends, or comparing major life costs, our goal is to make the math easier, faster, and more transparent.

We created WellSplit because many of the most stressful money questions are not really about math alone. They are about fairness, tradeoffs, unequal incomes, shared living, group spending, and the awkward conversations that happen when people need a system everyone can understand.

Our focus is on speed, simplicity, and usability. No unnecessary features, no clutter. Just clean tools that work instantly.

Who WellSplit is for

WellSplit is built for:

• Couples managing shared bills, rent, savings, or uneven incomes.

• Roommates trying to split rent, utilities, and shared household costs fairly.

• Friends settling group dinners, road trips, vacations, and shared purchases.

• People comparing high-stakes money decisions like moving, commuting, job offers, or living arrangements.

• Anyone who wants a practical fairness calculator instead of a messy spreadsheet.

Built for speed, simplicity, and everyday use.

This website is continuously improving to provide better tools for users worldwide.

About the Shared Housing All-In Split Calculator

This calculator combines the main monthly costs of shared housing into one payment plan for each room or household group.

Rent can be divided equally or by bedroom size. Utilities, internet, and shared household expenses can be divided by occupants or equally by room.

How to use it

  • Enter monthly rent and shared household expenses.
  • Choose how the rent portion should be divided.
  • Choose how utilities and shared costs should be divided.
  • Add each room and its number of occupants.
  • Enter room sizes when using the size-based rent method.
  • Calculate each room total and per-person payment.

Only include costs shared by the household. Personal subscriptions, private purchases, and individual debts should remain separate.

Shared Housing All-In Split FAQs

Helpful answers about combining rent and shared household expenses.

What is a shared housing all-in split calculator?

It combines rent and common household expenses into one monthly payment plan for roommates.

What costs can be included?

You can include rent, utilities, internet, and shared household expenses such as cleaning supplies.

How is the rent portion divided?

Base rent can be divided equally by room or according to bedroom size.

How are shared costs divided?

Utilities, internet, and household costs can be divided by person or equally by room.

Why separate rent from shared costs?

Bedroom value and household usage are different, so using separate methods can produce a fairer split.

Can couples use this calculator?

Yes. Enter the couple as one room with two occupants.

How is a couple's amount shown?

The calculator shows the room total and the equal per-person amount within that room.

Can room size affect the result?

Yes. Choose the room-size method for the rent portion.

Can I ignore room size?

Yes. Choose an equal rent split by room.

What counts as shared household costs?

Examples include cleaning products, paper goods, shared subscriptions, and basic household supplies.

Should groceries be included?

Only include groceries if everyone shares them consistently.

Can utilities be split by person?

Yes. This is often useful because more occupants usually create more usage.

Can shared costs be divided by room?

Yes. Choose the equal-by-room method if every room is treated as one household group.

Does the calculator support many roommates?

Yes. You can add up to 20 rooms or household groups.

Can I enter zero for a cost?

Yes. Leave any unused expense category at zero.

Do all payments add up to the full total?

Yes. The room totals equal all entered monthly costs, apart from minor display rounding.

Can I use different currencies?

Yes. Select the currency symbol you want displayed.

Can I copy the result?

Yes. Use the Copy Result button after calculating.

Should personal expenses be included?

No. Only include costs shared by the household.

Is the result legally binding?

No. It is an estimate for household discussions.

About the Work-From-Home Rent Adjustment Calculator

The WellSplit Work-From-Home Rent Adjustment Calculator helps roommates account for regular daytime use of shared household space.

Most of the rent remains equal. Only the selected WFH portion is divided according to each roommate's monthly work-from-home days.

How to use it

  • Enter the total monthly rent.
  • Add each roommate.
  • Choose the WFH-adjusted percentage.
  • Enter each roommate's monthly WFH days.
  • Calculate the updated payments.

This simple method works best when the normal rent split is equal and the household only wants a modest adjustment for regular home-office use.

Work-From-Home Rent Adjustment FAQs

Quick answers about WFH use and roommate rent.

What is a work-from-home rent calculator?

It adjusts part of the rent based on how often each roommate works from home.

How does it work?

Most rent stays equal. The selected WFH portion is divided by work-from-home days.

What counts as a WFH day?

A day when a roommate regularly works or studies from the rental home.

What WFH percentage should I use?

Many households use 5% to 20% of rent.

Why keep most rent equal?

Bedrooms and basic access remain available regardless of daytime use.

What if nobody works from home?

The full rent is divided equally.

What if everyone has the same WFH days?

The rent remains equal.

Can one roommate enter zero days?

Yes. They will not receive a share of the WFH-adjusted portion.

Should weekends be counted?

Only count weekends used as regular work-from-home days.

Should occasional emails count?

Usually not. Count meaningful home-working days.

Can students use this calculator?

Yes. Regular study-from-home days can be entered the same way.

Can part-time workers use it?

Yes. Enter their actual monthly WFH days.

Does it include utilities?

No. It adjusts rent only.

Should utilities be adjusted too?

They can be calculated separately if needed.

Can couples use this calculator?

Yes. Add each partner separately if they pay rent individually.

Can I add many roommates?

Yes. Add up to 20 roommates.

Do payments add up to the rent?

Yes, apart from minor display rounding.

Can I copy the result?

Yes. Use the Copy Result button.

Is WFH rent adjustment always necessary?

No. Use it only when the household agrees it is useful.

Is the result legally binding?

No. It is an estimate for roommate discussions.
